Frequently Asked

Are your tools open-source?

Yes, most of our tools are open-source under MIT License. Premium features may require a subscription for commercial use. You can view, modify, and distribute our open-source code as per the license terms.

Can I contribute to Blue Owl Code?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions from the security community. Visit our GitHub to check open issues, feature requests, and contribution guidelines. Significant contributors may receive free Pro subscriptions.

Where is the source code hosted?

All our open-source projects are hosted on GitHub. Enterprise customers may request private repository access for proprietary components.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Pro subscription anytime through your PayPal account. Cancellations take effect at the end of your billing cycle with no prorated refunds. Enterprise plans require 30-day notice.
